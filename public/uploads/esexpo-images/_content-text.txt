ESIE Discussion among Big Shots
    On April 1, 2026, the opening ceremony of the 14th Energy Storage International Conference and Expo (ESIE 2026) was held grandly at Capital International Exhibition & Convention Center. ESIE 2026 is co-sponsored by China Energy Storage Alliance, China Energy Research Society, and the Institute of Engineering Thermophysics, Chinese Academy of Sciences. ESIE 2026, themed "Digital Intelligence Leads Industry Revolution, Energy Storage Drives Energy Transformation", gathered over 1,000 participants at the opening ceremony including government authorities, scientific research institutes, grid companies, power generation groups, and leading enterprises across the energy storage industry chain. The opening ceremony was broadcasted online through platforms such as China.com, Xinhuanet.com, China Electron- ics News, and CNESA's WeChat Video Channel, with total viewers exceeding 2.6 million.
